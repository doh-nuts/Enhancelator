How to use:

  • Choose an item from the item list
  • Fill in required data in its corresponding field
  • Data will be calculated

The result will be displayed in a table, the shown result values are average values, given the nature of enhancing, the item might cost less than what represented or might cost x10 more!

What are Enchanted Gloves, Guzzling Pouch, Enhancer's Top/Bottoms?
The Checkbox is whether to take into account the item's effect or not, and the field is for its enhancement level

What is Hourly Rate?
An added value after cost calculation, it is a way to calculate total amount to charge someone if you are charging based on hourly rate

What is % Rate?
An added value after cost calculation, it is a way charge someone based on % relative to final cost

What is Target?
What level you want the item to be enhanced to

What are the colored rows in the result table?
Blue is least material cost.
Green is least total cost.

What is Prot?
Protection level

What is Actions?
Enhance attempts
